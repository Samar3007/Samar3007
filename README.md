<h2 align="center">🚀 About Me</h2>

I’m a Computer Engineering graduate with hands-on experience improving large language models (LLMs) through prompt design, response evaluation, and human-centric refinement. I’ve worked with multimodal systems across text, audio, and video, contributing to model alignment by defining evaluation criteria, refining outputs, and applying RLHF techniques. I focus on building practical AI systems that solve real-world problems, with a strong interest in making models more accurate, reliable, and human-like. I enjoy turning complex ideas into usable solutions and continuously exploring emerging technologies.
